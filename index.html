The Science Behind Microgreens

Small in size, but mighty in nutrients. Research shows microgreens are a true superfood.

Nutrient Density

Scientific studies have found that **microgreens contain up to 40 times higher levels of nutrients** than their mature vegetable counterparts. This includes essential vitamins like Vitamin C, E, and K.

Antioxidant Power

They are a rich source of **antioxidants and polyphenols**, which help fight free radicals in the body, reduce inflammation, and may lower the risk of chronic diseases.

Variety of Benefits

Each variety offers unique benefits. For example, **radish microgreens** are linked to improved heart health, while **broccoli microgreens** contain sulforaphane, a compound known for its anti-cancer properties.
